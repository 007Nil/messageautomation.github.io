Message Automation is an application build for automatically sending a sms based on the mode set. It will basically look for an incoming call and will auto send a sms to the incoming number. It is simple and easy to use.


----- About Privacy -----


We are very concern about the privacy of our users. We would like to inform all our users that this app do not store any kind of sensitive data of our users except for the user name.


The purpose of each permission is given below :


  • Phone - To detect any incoming calls in the device
  • Call logs - To detect the incoming call's phone number
  • SMS - To send sms to the caller of incoming call
  • Do not disturb - To manage device audio state
  • Notification - To show notification

This app is made in India.
